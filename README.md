PlantPal is a simple and friendly way to keep track of your house plants. It helps you remember when to water them so they stay happy and healthy!

## Features

-   **My Garden Dashboard**: See a quick overview of all your plants and which ones need attention.
-   **Add New Plants**: Easily add your plants with details like their name, type (Succulent, Tropical, etc.), location, and how often they need water.
-   **Watering Reminders**: The app tells you exactly when to water each plant.
    -   **Urgent**: Needs water now!
    -   **Healthy**: Good for a few more days.
-   **Track Care**: Click the "Water" ðŸ’§ or "Fertilize" ðŸŒŸ buttons when you take care of a plant to reset its schedule.
-   **Filter Views**: Quickly switch between viewing "All" plants, "Urgent" ones that need water, or "Healthy" ones.

## How to Use

Simply visit the hosted website (link available in the repository's **About** section). Existing on the web makes it easy to access your plants from anywhere without installing anything.

1.  Open the website in your browser.
2.  Start adding your plants!

## Note
Currently, PlantPal runs entirely in your browser memory. If you refresh the page, your plant list will reset.
